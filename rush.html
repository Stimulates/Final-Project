EXPECTION

Expect to be immediately involved with community service projects with the brothers, campus organizations, leadership programs, and teamwork building activities. Tau-Psi pledge process is 60 days.

REQUIRED GPA

The minimum GPA for pledging and initiation is determined by the chapter and the host institution. Every year we meet with our Greek Advisor and on campus official's so the GPA requirement may change. Any GPA questions you may have can be asked in our contact page.

FINANCIAL

During the pledge period, there are two necessary fees that must be paid to the Headquarters in Charlottesville, Virginia.

1. $45 pledging fee – one time fee due prior to the pledging ceremony
2. $150 initiation fee – one time fee due prior to the initiation ceremony
